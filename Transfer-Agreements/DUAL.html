Dual/Guaranteed Admission

Click on a category to view the list of schools.

Click on the name of the school to download the agreement file.

×

Core-to-Core: a schools promise to accept the general education credits embedded in a specific degree from DCCC. Students earning the A.A. or A.S. degree prior to transfer qualify for the Core-to-Core agreement. Depending on the transfer institution and intended major, students enrolled in an A.F.A. or A.A.S. degree may also qualify. Refer to the school's agreement for details on course information.

Dual Admission/Guaranteed Admission: guaranteed admission to the partner school. Students may also be guaranteed admission to a particular major of choice as long as eligibility requirements are met. In order to be eligible to transfer under the terms of the Dual Admission agreement, students must complete an Associate in Arts, Associate in Fine Arts, Associate in Science or Associate in Applied Science to qualify. The application fee will be waived or refunded. Students transferring under the Dual Admission program may also be guaranteed scholarships. Additional benefits may be included in the agreement.

Program-to-Program: Program-to-Program agreements are pathways, maps, or guides that indicate how the courses included in a DCCC major transfer to the partner school. These agreements provide guidance for the elective courses that students should complete prior to transfer, in order to transfer into junior year status.
